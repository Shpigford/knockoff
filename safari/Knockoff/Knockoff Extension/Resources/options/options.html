How detection works

Every listing's brand is checked against a curated register of roughly 5,000 established brands, refreshed daily. Unknown names are scored on the linguistic signature of trademark-squat brands: all-caps strings, vanishing vowels, unpronounceable consonant runs. Your lists above override everything. It all runs locally; the only network request is the daily list refresh from api.knockoff.shopping.
